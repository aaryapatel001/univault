About UniVault

A student-built platform to make academic resources accessible for every junior.

  • Curated assignments with solutions from all semesters
  • Previous Year Question (PYQ) papers with answer keys
  • Practice MCQs with instant feedback for exam preparation
  • Teacher-provided PPTs and official documents
  • Handwritten and typed study notes from toppers
  • Subject-wise organized content for quick access
